Park Ave Pub - Under new management! The Pub is focused on making the best customer experience as possible. The Pub does this by making our cocktails with fresh juice, source our kitchen ingredients from local stores and listening to customer feedback! Park Ave Pub frequently checks facebook and review sites to maintain what our community wants and likes! The Pub is a space that caters to groups of all ages and hopes to be the favourite spot to hangout and relax at!

The Pub has had some major redesigns in the front patio. The Pub has new tables for the outside back patio as well! Our new owner Robert is often found inside the Pub or here in town. The new rennovations inside the Pub give a more open feel to the space. Park Ave Pub often has live music and events centered around locals in Chico. The Pub has also made sure to keep up to date with doordash, so delivery is the next best option for great food if staying in is the mood for the day. The Pub has some amazing Bartenders always looking to show off their skills and make drinks anyone will enjoy! Park Ave Pub is also home to some of the best cooks in town that will have you wanting more!
